## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- ❌ **GUI Not Appearing After Execution**
  The interface may fail to render if your tool version is outdated. **Solution:** update your tool to the latest available release and re-attach to the Roblox process before executing the Sail Your Boat Script again.

- ❌ **Auto Farm Stops Collecting Resources**
  This typically happens when the game pushes a server-side loot table change. **Solution:** re-download the newest version of the Sail Your Boat Script, as the farming logic may need a patch to match updated spawn data.

- ❌ **Teleport Fails or Sends to Wrong Location**
  Map coordinate shifts after game updates can misalign teleport targets. **Solution:** clear your `WaypointList` in the config and let the script rebuild default coordinates on next launch.

- ❌ **Script Causes Noticeable Lag or Stuttering**
  Running too many features simultaneously on older hardware can cause frame drops. **Solution:** disable non-essential features like `NotificationAlerts` and lower `SpeedMultiplier` to `1.0` in your `config.lua` file.

- ❌ **Anti-AFK Not Preventing Disconnection**
  Some servers use aggressive timeout policies that bypass standard keep-alive signals. **Solution:** enable both `AntiAFK` and `AutoSail` together — the constant movement input provides a secondary activity signal.

- ❌ **Mobile GUI Overlapping Game Elements**
  On smaller screens the draggable panel may cover important buttons. **Solution:** long-press the GUI title bar and drag it to a corner, or toggle **Minimize Mode** from the settings tab within the panel.
